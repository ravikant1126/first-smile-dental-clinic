Restorative Dentistry

Dental Implants

Replace missing teeth with permanent artificial teeth.

⏱ 45–90 mins 📅 4–6 Visits 😌 Minimal Pain 🦷 Healing 3–6 Months
Book

Overview

  • Permanent replacement for missing teeth.
  • Titanium implant placed inside jaw.
  • Looks and feels like a natural tooth.

Symptoms

  • Missing tooth
  • Difficulty chewing
  • Loose dentures
  • Bone loss

Procedure

  1. Consultation
  2. X-ray / Scan
  3. Implant Surgery
  4. Healing (3–6 months)
  5. Crown Placement

Quick Facts

  • Visits : 4–6
  • Surgery : 45–90 min
  • Recovery : 3–7 days
  • Healing : 3–6 months

Benefits

  • Natural look
  • Strong chewing
  • Long lasting
  • Protects jaw bone

Aftercare

  • Brush twice daily
  • Floss regularly
  • Avoid smoking
  • Visit dentist regularly
