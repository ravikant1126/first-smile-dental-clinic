When should you see a dentist for tooth pain?

Tooth pain that lingers, wakes you at night, or comes with swelling should be checked promptly. Early diagnosis can often make treatment simpler.

How often should children visit a dentist?

Many children benefit from regular checkups so cavities, brushing issues and tooth eruption can be monitored early.

Cleaning vs whitening: what is the difference?

Cleaning removes deposits and stains from the tooth surface. Whitening is a separate cosmetic process to improve tooth shade after dental evaluation.
